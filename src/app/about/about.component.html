About Pomodoro Pets

Welcome to Pomodoro Pets, your productivity companion that combines the effective Pomodoro Technique with adorable virtual pets. Our unique twist on the classic timer helps you stay focused and motivated throughout your work sessions.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.[1] It uses a kitchen timer to break work into intervals, typically 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for tomato, after the tomato-shaped kitchen timer Cirillo used as a university student. -Wikipedia

How to Use Pomodoro Pets

  • Choose your pet companion
  • Set your work interval (default is 25 minutes)
  • Set your short and long break intervals (defaults are 5 and 15 minutes respectively)
  • Set up the number of cycles for short/ long breaks (default is 4)
  • Click start button to begin your Pomodoro session
  • Work until the timer rings
  • Take a timed break
  • Repeat the cycle

Pomodoro Pet Features

  • Customizable work and break intervals
  • Choice of various pet companions
  • Various alarm sound options
